About

This project solves the discrete log problem \( h=g^x \mod p \). Rather than brute forcing, it splits x into two components and runs a meet-in-the-middle attack. \( \frac{h}{g^{x_1}}=(g^B)^{x_0} \) We don't verify that p is indeed prime.
